"I had this idea and I think it could make a lot of money. I know how to produce the product but I don't know how to sell it. Where would I even start?"

Do the numbers! is an online tool that takes the mystery out of starting a new business. The system will guide you step by step through each stage of the process starting with testing the credibility of the idea, following through to building a case for investors and finally getting your product to market.

The website will also have additional resources available for those who have already started the journey to success and are looking for supporting information. Resources would be available through the site that would benefit start ups and established businesses such as:

  • Tax return guides
  • Turnover/profit calculators
  • Marketing wizards

There's a lot of energy behind the entrepreneurial movement at the moment and DO the numbers! is the way to make everyone a part of it.
